About Us

ETS2MCG(ETS2 Multiplayer Convoys Group) Team was established in 2016. We are an Asian Team with many countries: Vietnam, Thailand, Korea, Japan, Indonesia, Malaysia, China.

  • Friendly, active and fun community for all drivers.
  • Trips and Weekly Convoys.
  • Strong Staff and Friendly Management.
  • 300+ Drivers.
  • We play both ETS2 and ATS.
ETS2MCG is not only a simple team but also a big family for all drivers.

Why should you choose ETS2MCG?

First of all, you will have many chances to drive together. We hold trips(a few people driving together every evening) almost everyday, which you can join whenever you want. You don't have to join them often to remain in the team. Just join whenever you want.

Don't worry if you love playing with a lot of people, such as in large convoys. We also join or organize large events about once a week.

Almost every event that we join is held in evening, the best time to take some rest after working hard. If you live in Asia and love driving together with many players, we're sure ETS2MCG's the perfect place for you. Don't worry if you live in other regions. If you're European and want to drive in afternoon, you can join us too. We don't have country restrictions.

Furthermore, you will have benefits like these:

  1. You can apply for a DLC gift event.
  2. You can quickly check and participate in events within and outside our team.
  3. You can play games with your team members and build friendships.
  4. You can experience our team's unique fast driving!

Applying for ETS2MCG

If you meet all of the requirements below, you may apply for ETS2MCG member.

  • You are at least 14 years old.
  • Your attitude towards others is positive and friendly.
  • You have to play ETS2 for at least 100 hours.
  • Regularly join us in convoys/events.
  • Change your name on TruckersMP to: [MCG] Your name after you join ETS2MCG.
  • Usually active in the team and Discord.
  • You must always abide by TruckersMP rules and drive in a responsible manner.
  • Make sure you don't have more than 3 bans on TruckersMP's profile.
